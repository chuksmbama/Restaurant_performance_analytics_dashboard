# Restaurant_performance_analytics_dashboard
## Overview
This Power BI dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of restaurant performance, including sales trends, pricing patterns, customer distribution, and order behavior across multiple cities and channels. It enables business stakeholders to make data-driven decisions for growth, profitability, and customer engagement.

## Objective
- Track key performance metrics such as Sales, Orders, Average Price, and Customers
- Visualize month-over-month (MoM) growth and identify top-performing products and cities
- Analyze purchase methods and types to optimize marketing and pricing strategies
- Present insights through an intuitive, interactive dashboard

## Insights
- Top Product: Bugers ($347,952)
- Top City: Lisbon ($211, 749)
- Top payment method: Credit Card (47%)
- Top Channel: in-Store (42%)

## Observations
- Strong growth driven by in-store and credit card purchases
- Slight price drop helped boost total orders
- High volume of ordders from october-December
- Strong engagment from lisbon and london

## Tools and Techniques
- Power BI - Dasbaord creation, DAX measures, data modeling
- SQL: Data storage, data cleaning and preprocessing
